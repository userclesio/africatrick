What You Will Discover Inside

The Nigerian Root Trick is a carefully researched educational guide that documents the traditional preparation and cultural use of Bulbine natalensis — a root central to male wellness rituals in West and Southern African traditional medicine systems for centuries.

Written in accessible English for a global audience, this e-book compiles ancestral recipes, preparation methods, and historical protocols as recorded from indigenous healers across Nigeria, Ghana, and South Africa.

Meet the Author

Kwame Obeng

Kwame Obeng is an independent South African ethnobotanist who has dedicated over 18 years to documenting and preserving traditional African medicinal plant knowledge before it is lost to modernisation.

Having worked alongside traditional healers — izinyanga and izangoma — across Nigeria, Ghana, and South Africa, Kwame has compiled an extensive archive of ancestral wellness practices, root preparation methods, and ritual protocols passed down orally through generations. His research spans over 240 plant species and three countries.

"This knowledge belongs to Africa. My mission is simply to translate it into a language the world can access — without stripping away its cultural soul."
